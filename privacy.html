BRIK

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

BRIK is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to operate the app and improve your experience. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Data We Collect

When you use BRIK the following data is collected and stored securely on our servers:

  • Anonymous user identifier (generated automatically, not linked to your name or email)
  • Workout session records (day theme, equipment mode, blocks completed, duration)
  • Block performance data (rounds completed, difficulty rating)
  • App version and device day of week
  • Fitness level and equipment preferences

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Workout history detail and exercise lists
  • Achievements unlocked
  • Streak and weekly progress
  • Duration and theme preferences
  • Tutorial completion status

How We Use Your Data

Data collected is used solely to:

  • Power workout logging and session tracking features
  • Improve workout programming and generator quality
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the app

Anonymous Authentication

BRIK uses anonymous authentication. No email address, name, or personal identifying information is required or collected. You are assigned a random anonymous identifier when you first open the app.

Third Party Services

BRIK uses Supabase to store workout session data securely. Supabase is a trusted infrastructure provider. Your data is stored on Supabase servers and is subject to Supabase's security standards. We do not share your data with any other third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not use advertising or third party analytics tracking
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts

Data Retention

Session data stored on our servers is retained to power your workout history and progression features. Local device data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. To request deletion of your server-side data email us at support@getbrik.app.

Children's Privacy

BRIK is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app please contact us at support@getbrik.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the app. Continued use of BRIK after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at support@getbrik.app
