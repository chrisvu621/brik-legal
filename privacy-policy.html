Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

BRIK does not collect any personal data. All workout data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your phone.

Data We Store

The following data is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage:

— Equipment preferences

— Workout history and session records

— Achievements unlocked

— Duration and theme preferences

Data We Do NOT Collect

— We do not collect personal information

— We do not use analytics or tracking

— We do not share data with third parties

— We do not require account creation

— We do not have access to your data

Data Deletion

All data can be deleted by uninstalling the app from your device.
